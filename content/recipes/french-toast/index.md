ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 2 eggs
- 2 tbsp ground cinnamon
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 pinches of salt
- 4 pieces of bread

directions:
- Beat eggs, milk, salt, cinnamon, & vanilla
- Heat lightly oiled pan on medium
- Soak bread thoroughly on both sides
- Cook until light brown and crisp on both sides
